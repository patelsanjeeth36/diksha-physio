What I Offer

Specialised Physiotherapy Services

From acute injuries to chronic pain management, I offer a comprehensive range of physiotherapy treatments designed to restore your function and wellbeing.

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Treatment for joint pain, fractures, back & neck pain, arthritis, and post-fracture recovery to restore full movement and strength.

Sports Injury Recovery

Rapid, targeted rehabilitation for sprains, ligament tears, muscle strains, and overuse injuries — helping athletes return to peak performance.

Post-Surgery Rehabilitation

Structured recovery plans after knee replacements, shoulder surgeries, spinal procedures, and other operations to maximise your surgical outcomes.

Neurological Physiotherapy

Specialised care for stroke, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and spinal cord injuries — focusing on improving mobility and independence.

Chronic Pain Management

Holistic, long-term strategies to manage fibromyalgia, chronic back pain, frozen shoulder, and other persistent pain conditions.

Home Visit Physiotherapy

Convenient, professional physiotherapy sessions in the comfort of your own home — ideal for elderly patients, post-surgery recovery, or mobility-limited individuals.
